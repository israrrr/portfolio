03 — Context

Hospitality / Strategy & Identity

Veenu - Premium Vegetarian Restaurant

Founded as a tribute to a mother's legacy, VEENU is a premium highway-side vegetarian restaurant on the Indore-Gujarat transit corridor. It was built to elevate roadside dining from a purely functional pitstop into an experience of genuine warmth and architectural precision.

04 — The Problem

A 3-Second Window.

The brand faced a dual perception barrier. Locally, VEENU had to shed its former small-café identity to establish a premium reality. For transit travelers, the challenge was interception: convincing them to bypass a 10-year entrenched incumbent at 80 km/h, relying entirely on a 3-second visual trust signal.

05 — The Strategy

Compete on Trust.

We shifted the battleground from subjective food claims to absolute operational care. The strategy engineered an identity that communicated the warmth of a mother's kitchen through an architect's rigorous discipline. The core promise: Home, Served.

06 — The Decisions

Architectural Signal.

Rejected

Corporate hospitality jargon like 'culinary journeys', and the saturated, chaotic orange noise used by every generic dhaba on the corridor.

Accepted

Speaking directly to the guest. A dominant Earthen Brown ground projects permanence, reserving Saffron purely for emotional, appetite-trigger moments.

07 — Ideation

Mapping the Landscape.

A 10km corridor visual audit revealed an entire category shouting in the same colors. We systematically stripped away decorative noise to engineer a mark rooted in the brand's phonetic core: V (We), EEN (The Connection), U (You).

08 — The Mark

The
Mark.

Two mirrored, incomplete circular forms reaching toward each other—symbolizing physical separation and enduring care. Tracing the contour reveals an infinity loop, mathematically reduced to its absolute minimum anchor points to read perfectly from 200 meters away.

13 — Context

Asset Management / Brand Identity Design

Bakkengrow - Asset Management Identity

Asset management on a guaranteed-principal model, three young founders possessed the technical acumen of veterans but the visual presence of a DIY startup. They needed the immediate institutional gravity of a legacy wealth management firm.

14 — The Problem

The Triple Trust Deficit.

The brand had to overcome severe category skepticism surrounding crypto in India, an age-related credibility barrier for young founders managing savings, and a hard growth ceiling reliant entirely on personal networks.

15 — The Strategy

Reframing Fear.

We reframed crypto from a speculative gamble into a structured responsibility. By strictly targeting a duty-motivated audience, we positioned the brand as a managed path to financial stability. The load-bearing promise: Protected Capital.

16 — The Decisions

Dual-Register Voice.

Rejected

"Empowerment over risk" (which inadvertently highlights fear) and generic fintech gradients.

Accepted

Giving freedom by means of a dual-register verbal system. "Be A Breadwinner" for consumer aspiration, and "Managed Capital. Secured Principal." for uncompromising institutional authority.

17 — Ideation

Finding the Open Quadrant.

A 2x2 market audit revealed the "finance-traditional + conservative" quadrant was completely unoccupied. We abandoned the chaotic crypto color spectrum to engineer a system with centuries of institutional weight: Deep Purple and Muted Gold.

18 — The Mark

The Master Mark.

A three-layer master mark seamlessly integrating quotation marks (deliberate insight), origami geometry (shaping capital), and a foundational circular grid mathematically engineered for a future proprietary token launch.
